What is Mumble?

Mumble is group voice chat! It's like a Skype call that's always going on, and players can come and go from.

It's great for helping coordinate projects in the world.

Here's how you set it up:

  1. Download the Mumble client here: Desktop Android iOS
  2. Run through the setup wizard to get your microphone and speakers configured
  3. Connect to mumble.awholenew.world and pick a username
