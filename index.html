The History of New World

New World Gaming Group is the spiritual successor to Raxis Prime, a Minecraft server established in 2011.


Raxis Prime was created in the summer of 2011 as a way for a friends group to collaborate and build together. v1 of the server lasted just three days, as everyone had unlimited items and flight. This quickly devolved into chaos; Everyone had admin privileges, lava flowed everywhere, people destroyed each other's works, and chaos reigned.

That is, until Raxis Prime v2 launched. We quickly archived our first world and started a new world; One with players in Survival Mode instead of Creative, where we would have to work together to collect resources instead of simply having them. This world lasted for a few months, until around September of 2011. Newer versions of Minecraft had been released, and we wanted to start anew.

Download Raxis Prime v2

When we created Raxis Prime v3, we wanted the spawn point to be more than just a list of rules. A village was built that the player would need to walk through. This village had a community room, underground enchantment room facility, trading room, lookout tower, and more. It eventually also housed the prized Enderdragon egg as a trophy to our victory over the mighty Enderdragon. We built in this world until around March of 2012, when we decided to refresh the world. New updates to Minecraft meant that if we restarted our world anew, we would have new blocks to work with. So we did!

Download Raxis Prime v3

Raxis Prime v4 started players out on a giant platform in the sky. They had to jump down into the ocean, swim to a nearby continent, and make their way to a small welcome center on the nearby island. From there, they could teleport to one of the four corners of the map and begin their journey. This world lived and evolved for over a year, featuring a giant tree, castles, dungeons, and much more. During this time, we also had a secondary server. This server had an always-regenerating automated Hunger Games arena. By the summer of 2013, however, interest had died down and the worlds became barren. The difficult decision was made to shut down the server on August 1, 2013.
